Celeste: A Journey of Self-Discovery

Celeste isn't just a game; it's an emotional journey wrapped in stunning pixel art and brilliant level design. You play as Madeline, a young woman determined to climb the treacherous Celeste Mountain. What makes this game unforgettable isn't just the tight platforming mechanics—though they're incredible—but its thoughtful exploration of mental health, self-doubt, and perseverance.
